The Similarities Between the Video Game Crash and the Battle Royale Craze

The Video Game Crash of 1983 was a massive failure for the video game market. It brought about disaster to the genre of entertainment and almost killed it entirely. The Battle Royale genre of video games shares many similarities with the events leading to the video game crash of 1983. I propose that if the Battle Royale craze were to increase in another wave, it could bring about events that could cause the entire genre to crash. Throughout this site, I will publish evidence and facts to allow you to draw your own conclusions about the similarities the two forms of entertainment share.
